# The History of Bitcoin

From the 2008 whitepaper by Satoshi Nakamoto to global adoption, Bitcoin introduced digital scarcity and decentralized money.

Milestones:

- 2009: Genesis block mined
- 2010: First real-world purchase (10,000 BTC for pizza)
- 2017: SegWit activates, paving the way for scaling
- Today: A global asset and settlement network
